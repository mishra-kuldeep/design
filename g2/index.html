About

G2 Accounting

G2 Accounting is the work of brothers David and Alan Giffard, and is the Melbourne-based extension of DSG Accounting which is based in Bendigo. The boys grew up on a dairy farm in northern Victoria. In recent years, the practice has gone 'gangbusters' and has now made the move into Melbourne in the classy inner-suburb of Kew. That's why we've had to poach some of the finest accountants and admin staff going around, adding Jess, Kara, Prue and Belinda to the mix.
