How it works

Edventuroz was designed to make internships abroad more accessible and readily available for aspiring new talented students. We understand everyone is very different and encourage you to reach out privately so we can create the best possible service for you. Here’s the main stages of our internship process!

1
MEET & GREET

Book a free consultation online now by filling out our enquiry form. One of our Globe Guru’s will reach out and organize a Skype so we can get to know each other. We’ll conduct a small interview, take your resume so we can prepare our feedback and get some details about your internship so we can look into your options. (look into resume courses)

2
JOIN OUR NETWORK

Once we’ve had a chance to get to know each other and we know we can achieve your goals we’ll ask you for a deposit so you can join our intern program. Once the deposit has been paid we’ll give you an in depth breakdown of your interview and send you our recommendations to strengthen your resume.

3
ACCEPT AN OFFER

Once your resume is ready to get you the best possible results we’ll begin searching our huge network of employers to find you a perfect match. We’ll do all the hard work and once we’ve found a match we’ll give you a call to fill you in on all the details. Accept the internship by signing the contract and paying the remaining amount.

4
PREPARATION

Now it’s time to get excited. You’ll need to book your flights, organize a visa, get some travel insurance and plan your travels post internship. At Edventuroz we have an entire team who can do this for you. Speak with your ambassador to find out more.

5
TRAINING

In order to get you off to the best possible start you will undergo some more one on one training with your Globe Guru. This training will be on your location, your job role and your employer. You’ll have everything you need to hit the ground running.

6
TAKE THE STEP

Start your certified FRESH internship and open up new doors for your future!
