why choose us

Knowledgeable Expert

Our team has been carefully hand selected so we can give you the best possible advice and support. Our Globe Guru’s have either travelled the world or searched for and undertaken an internship in a foreign country. When you decide to travel with us, you will have one dedicated contact throughout. We believe it’s important for us to know your adventure inside out so your Globe Guru will design your tour, book the services and be at the end of the phone from start to finish.

Lifetime Membership

We’ll be with you the whole way as every one of our customers gets everlasting help from our team. Whether it's future travel planning, access to our job boards, help building your CV for that perfect application or general career advice we have you covered.

Life Changing Experiences

We are here to give you the ultimate life experience. We rely on our industry experience, supplier knowledge and tourism networks to ensure your travel experience surpasses your expectations. We are always looking for those new opportunities to change your lives.

We won’t break the bank

Competitively priced student and internship adventure travel packages. We will offer a price match on all tours and placements, please see our T&C’s. Pay for it your way with a diverse range of payment plans available! Just a small deposit is the beginning of the adventure.

Tailor made for you

We understand that everyone is different and we understand that this is your life. Edventuroz are with you from start to finish allowing us to understand all of your travel and educational needs and preferences. Your Globe Guru will know your tour inside out, from designing a customized itinerary, booking the services and being at the end of the phone should you need them when on your Edventure!

For every student that books with us we will plant a tree in Australia to help recover forests affected by the 2019-20 Black Summer Bushfires and restore native habitat vital for wildlife.
