Who we are

WHO WE ARE

Bold, Refreshing, Quality, Different & Cool. Australia & New Zealand Student adventure specialists.

The name Edventuroz derives from our obsessions and passions on which this company was created. Education and learning, adventures and tourism, Australia & New Zealand! Ed-Ventur-Oz!

Our founder has first-hand experience in the hospitality, travel and tourism sectors stretching over 15 years, across 3 continents and numerous countries. Edventuroz was forged from a deep passion of making life changing experiences abroad more accessible for everyone. We firmly believe everybody is entitled to the chance of being able to reach their full potential through education whilst being able to experience what the world has to offer. Edventuroz was created to do exactly as the name suggests: to consistently deliver outstanding student adventure travel and internship offerings in the most sought after destinations the world has to offer.

Edventuroz is a refreshing new Student & internship adventure travel agency that aims to organise single or group student travel including tours, activities, accommodation, transport and of course internships! We are constantly striving towards creating life changing memories and believe there is no better way to learn and discover than to get out there and explore the world.

Not only does Edventuroz create trips of a lifetime but it specializes in hospitality, tourism and travel internships and is focused on not only relieving the pressures of the internship process on the student but also their prospective employers. Edventuroz will source, vet, train and approve students before enrolling, training and implementing them into their new roles at zero cost and minimal effort to the employers so they can focus on growing their business.

Edventuroz aims to become industry recognized as the premium travel and tourism internship agency. Our dream is to have The Edventuroz internship program acknowledged by employers as a world class service giving our interns a competitive edge in the job market. We are confident that we can place any intern in any job role so even if you aren’t a hospitality, tourism or travel student please reach out to us.

At Edventuroz we have started from the bottom and through hard work, determination, a lot of highs and lows, we’ve come out the other end with a wealth of knowledge, unbelievable experiences and a burning desire to give young students the stepping stones they deserve to a better future. We’ve studied at colleges and universities, travelled the world, completed internships and also managed interns in previous job roles. All of these experiences allow us to confidently say we will give you the best advice and guidance to complete your adventure of a lifetime.

After witnessing the sacrifices, trust and finances students put in others and having heard many nightmare stories of student travel and internships gone wrong we decided to start our own agency where our students get the best care from start to finish. We want to ride the journey with you and will be there after your travels have been completed to help you with the next phase of your life. Our founder has spent the last 7 years working for the largest youth tourism organization in the southern hemisphere, he has an extensive network of suppliers and operators in one of the most desirable places in the world, Australia. He has interviewed, hired and trained over 150 interns over the past 36 months and has a passion for seeing them grow, gain life experience, complete their education and go onto bigger and better things. Are you ready to take that Edventuroz step into your future?
