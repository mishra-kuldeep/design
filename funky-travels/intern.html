why intern?

WHY INTERN?

Internships allow you to gain valuable work experience with an organisation that will also provide you with guidance and feedback allowing you to grow into a highly employable person. Completing internships also gives you the opportunity to develop professional contacts and networks.

Internships provide a great environment to meet professionals in the career field you want to pursue, as well as other interns who have similar interests. Internships give you a real insight into different management styles and the pressures of everyday tasks.

Completing an internship is a fantastic way to apply everything you have been taught in your chosen academic field. Internships are a great way to apply the knowledge from the classroom to real-world experience. Learning is one thing, but taking those skills into the workforce and applying them is a great way to explore different career paths and departments that suit your separate interests.

Internships are a great way to gain incredible insight into a company’s missions and goals and provides you with information about company and or industry rules, regulations and procedures.

Internships are an incredible way to gain valuable experience and increase your marketability. Internships help students learn all about workplace culture, employee relations, and leadership structure.

By completing an internship you gain a critical edge over other candidates when applying for jobs. Most organizations and jobs that you apply for will want employees to have some sort of professional experience. If you get through to the interview stage and haven’t had an internship experience but the others have, you may lose out on that job opportunity. It also prepares you for what to expect in your chosen career and increases confidence in your work and abilities.

Interning is a great way to learn new skills and observe other professionals in their job role to learn the ins and outs of different positions. Internships will allow you to figure out what you like and don’t like. While everyone probably wants to walk away from an internship feeling excited and passionate about the experience, there’s a silver-lining to be found if you didn’t enjoy the job: you’ll know what you don’t like.

Why intern Abroad

Like any internship experience, an internship abroad will help you enhance your professional skills and improve your resume. An international internship will differentiate you from others on your resume and will give you that competitive edge when over others that have interned when applying for jobs.

Doing an internship in another country is a big challenge. It can be scary, but all the frustrations and challenges you’ll face abroad and within your international internship will help you gain confidence in your own abilities. You should do an internship abroad because you want to travel and see new cultures, beautiful sights, meet new people and create life changing memories.

We are living in an international job market and having a world perspective will help you relate to people in and outside of the workplace. Having global awareness will strengthen your soft skills like communication and positive attitude.

Anyone who has lived or interned abroad will tell you that nothing makes you learn faster than by immersing yourself in a new country. You will quickly adapt to understand the nuances of a new culture, language, and even workplace behavior.

Deciding to go and live abroad for a few months can be a daunting, but exciting, decision. However, once you are there and interning on your own it gives you a very real sense of independence and empowerment.

It’s hard to tell if you really love your chosen career field until you experience what it’s like working in it on a day-to-day basis. If it ends up that you need to reevaluate what you want to focus on after graduation at least you still gained some great transferable skills and you got to travel.

You can never have too many people in your network. During your internship abroad you will make connections with your host employer, other interns, and other leaders within your industry overseas.

An internship abroad can be described as a lot of things such as: demanding, life changing, thrilling, nerve-wracking, but it will also always be fun. An internship abroad is a fun adventure filled with new skills, breathtaking destinations, lasting friendships, and lots of life long memories.

Why Australia

Australia is an incredible destination to complete an internship and has huge amounts of tours and activities to offer!

Australia provides excellent opportunities for university students and young professionals to gain international work experience. By taking part in an internship in Australia you will have the opportunity to meet industry professionals and expand your global network.

Interning abroad in Australia will provide you with a completely integrated experience. Edventuroz offers lots of destinations to choose from. We have positions available in the big cities, in rural landscapes, and stunning beach resorts, you can explore so many different adventures during your internship in Australia.

During free time away from your Australia internship, you will be able to take advantage of everything this incredible country has to offer. Some awesome activities you can do are going on Fraser Island tours, Skydiving mission beach or scuba diving the Great Barrier Reef. Our Globe Gur’s will help you create your own incredible edventuroz experience.
