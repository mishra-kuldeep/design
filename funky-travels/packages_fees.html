Packages & Fees

PACKAGES & FEES

When you choose to intern with Edventuroz, we arrange everything for you. We’ll secure a fantastic internship position for you with an innovative organization that matches with your interests, supports your long-term career goals and ticks off your educational needs. You’ll also benefit from our excellent support services that will grow your transferable skills and give you solid foundations for future employment.

These services are all included in all of our basic internship programmes.

  • Personality test
  • Interview feedback
  • Resume builder
  • Internship placement
  • Airport pick up and drop off after internship.
  • Sim Card
  • Tax File Number
  • Bank Card
  • Superannuation application
  • Assistance with tax and superannuation refund
  • 2nd Year Visa and migration help
  • Discounted training courses (RSA/White card etc)
  • Upto 3 hours of 1 to 1 training on your new employer and your selected destination
  • A free Travel consultation after your internship so you can explore Australia.
  • Life long access to our job board
  • Life long newsletters

It’s free to apply for an internship with Edventuroz. Once we have reviewed your application and ticked you off against our acceptance checkpoints, you’ll need to pay a deposit of 250EURO to confirm your place. The remaining balance of your Program Fee (less your initial 250EURO deposit payment) will be due no less than 60 days before your scheduled start date. Our basic internship programs are a flat fee of 750EURO.

NOT INCLUDED IN OUR BASIC PROGRAM

  • Tourism/leisure activities during free time. Book with us now for exclusive discounts.
  • Personal spending money to cover basic expenses such as drinks, snacks, laundry, and public transportation.
  • Accommodation, Flights, Visa,and Travel.

travel

Flights

We have exclusive access to all of the best airfare deals. We can help you find the best flights to fit around your internships.

Visas

Our Globe Guru’s can take the stress out of the visa application. Allow us to apply for your visa and do all the paperwork.

Accommodation

Some of our internships include shared accommodation at no extra cost but these are limited and are given to those applicants who are particularly studying hospitality, travel and or tourism. We have private accommodation offerings that start at just 150EURO per week. We have sourced lots of different accommodation offerings available to suit all of your needs.
